Company Cluster

The purpose of this visualization application is to provide an overview of the different companies and sectors
within the U.S. Dividends Champions, Challengers, and Contenders dataset provided by http://www.dripinvesting.org/Tools/Tools.asp.

Each bubble represents a company, and each color identifies which sector they are within. The size of each bubble is relative to
each company's market capitalization. This makes it easy to identify the large companies and the smaller ones. Exploring the
visualization is easy, with the ability to get information about each bubble by simply hovering your mouse. This provides a
snapshot of some key attributes such as their market capitalization, estimated growth, past growth, and more.
